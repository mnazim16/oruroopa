Oruroopa.org is a non-profit organization aimed at providing education and better facility to the underprivileged children by the generous contribution of 1 Rupee every month. Our organization is focused primarily and absolutely on bringing about a revolution that bridges the gap of the “Educational Inequality” which, unfortunately, looms large in our country. We firmly believe that quality education is the only way to deal with issues that haunts our India. Therefore it is imperative to create a generation of educated, rational, humane minds who can take revolutionize our country as the leaders of tomorrow.

Concept

“The journey of a million miles begins with a single step”. To those who underestimate the value of a rupee coin in this world of inflation, here is the time to look at a brighter and bigger picture. Our organization expects a generous contribution of Re.1 per month from every student of each school. Hence , each student contributes rs.12 every year.
Each school/club/other institution will have to register for the “ A Rupee Revolution” program . A team will be chosen every year from each school for the smooth running of the organization. Same team will collect money from the students each month and will be kept under the custody of the school. A meeting to be held with the school members and the head team every 3 month for the assessment of the work. The collected money will be transferred to the head committee , monthly.
Once the collected money reaches the organization, the complete accounts will be showed in our website. School wise collection list will be send to all the schools participating in the campaign, every month. Collected money will be aimed at providing education and better facility to the underprivileged children. Applications will be accepted every year by students aiming for higher education and the received applications will be filtered on the basis of merit and financial background.
